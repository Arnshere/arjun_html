facts about dogs

The dog (Canis lupus familiaris) is a domesticated mammal and a subspecies of the gray wolf, known for being a popular,loyal, and highly trainable pet with over 60 major breeds. Descended from wolves during the Late Pleistocene, they serve various roles including companionship, herding, hunting, and working in police or rescue services

Dogs possess highly developed senses, including a superior sense of smell and the ability to see better in dim light than humans. They range in weight from 1.36 to 113.38 kg.
